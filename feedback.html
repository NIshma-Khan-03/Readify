How does the reading tracker work?

The reading tracker helps you monitor your reading progress. Simply enter the total pages of your book, how many pages you've read, and your daily reading speed. Our calculator will show you what percentage you've completed and estimate when you'll finish the book!

Can I save my book recommendations?

Absolutely! When you get a random book recommendation, simply click the "Save to List" button. Your reading list is saved in your browser, so you can come back to it anytime!

Is my data stored securely?

All your data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This means your information stays on your device and is not sent to any servers. Your privacy is our priority!

How do I search for specific books?

Head to the Book Explorer page and use the search bar at the top. You can search by book title or author name. You can also filter books by genre and sort them by title, author, or rating!

What are the ambient sounds for?

The Reading Flow page offers cozy ambient sounds to create the perfect reading atmosphere. Choose from rain, fireplace, coffee shop, ocean waves, forest, or thunder sounds to enhance your reading experience!

Can I use Readify on my mobile device?

Yes! Readify is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. Enjoy your reading companion anywhere, anytime!
